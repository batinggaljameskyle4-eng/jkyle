GOOD HEALTH AND WELL-BEING

United Nations Sustainable Development Goal 3 (Philippines)

Intro Goal Importance PH Context Programs Action

Introduction

Good health and well-being are essential for a productive and happy life. SDG 3 focuses on ensuring healthy lives and promoting well-being for all ages. It addresses major health concerns such as diseases, maternal health, and access to healthcare services.

Goal

SDG 3 aims to end epidemics, reduce maternal and child mortality, and ensure universal access to quality healthcare. It promotes healthier lifestyles, better nutrition, and safe environments for everyone.

Importance of Health and Well-Being

  • Healthy citizens contribute to economic growth and social stability.
  • Access to healthcare prevents diseases and saves lives.
  • Promoting mental health improves overall quality of life.
  • Good health enables education, work, and active community participation.

Philippine Situation

The Philippines has made progress in improving healthcare services and reducing child mortality. However, challenges remain in ensuring equal access to healthcare, addressing malnutrition, and improving mental health awareness.

Government and Health Programs

  • Universal Health Care (UHC) Act: Ensures that all Filipinos have access to quality and affordable healthcare services.
  • National Immunization Program: Provides free vaccines to prevent infectious diseases.
  • Mental Health Act: Promotes mental wellness and access to mental health services.
  • Nutrition Programs: Addresses hunger and malnutrition among children and mothers.

Take Action

We can all support SDG 3 by staying active, eating nutritious food, avoiding harmful substances, and supporting community health programs. Together, we can build a healthier and happier Philippines for everyone.
